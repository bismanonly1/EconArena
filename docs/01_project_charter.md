# Project Charter — MarketPulse AI

## Project Name
MarketPulse AI

## Objective
Build an AI-powered trading simulation app where users trade simulated assets while AI-generated news dynamically changes market prices.

## Problem
Beginners do not understand how news, macro events, and market psychology affect asset prices.

## Solution
Create a simulation where AI generates market events, the market reacts, and users learn by trading safely.

## Target Users
- Beginner investors
- Finance students
- AI/ML learners
- Trading simulation users

## MVP Features
- Simulated assets
- Buy/sell trading
- Portfolio tracking
- AI-generated news
- Market price changes
- AI explanation panel

## Out of Scope
- Real-money trading
- Brokerage integration
- Crypto wallets
- Mobile app
- Multiplayer

## Success Criteria
- User can trade assets
- Prices change after news
- Portfolio updates correctly
- AI explains market movement
- App is demo-ready
