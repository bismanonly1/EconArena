# Business Requirements Document — MarketPulse AI

## Business Need
Users need a safe and interactive way to understand trading, market reactions, and macroeconomic events.

## Product Vision
MarketPulse AI helps users learn trading by simulating real-world market situations through AI-generated news and dynamic market behavior.

## Business Goals
- Teach trading concepts
- Improve financial literacy
- Create an engaging simulation
- Demonstrate AI engineering skills

## User Problems
1. Users do not know why prices move.
2. Users are afraid of losing real money.
3. Existing trading simulators are not interactive enough.
4. Beginners need explanations, not just charts.

## MVP Business Requirements
- The app must show simulated assets.
- The app must allow users to buy and sell.
- The app must generate AI news events.
- The app must update prices based on events.
- The app must explain market movement.

## Success Metrics
- User can complete one full trading session.
- System generates at least 10 market events.
- Portfolio value updates correctly.
- AI explanation is understandable to beginners.
